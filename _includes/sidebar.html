The Codex Creativity in Vitro

The Codex is more than a blog. It is a living, evolving manuscript.
It is the lab book, the dream journal, the autopsy report, and the resurrection table of a speculative experiment called Creativity In Vitro.

Developed by Mad Scientists Lina Lopes and Eduardo Padilha, it explores the thin membrane between thought and form, between signal and symbol.

Inside the Codex you will find:
  • Notes from the Live Lab, where we tinker with EEG signals, datasets, neural networks, and creative feedback loops.
  • Logs of machine learning experiments and sensor testing
  • Documentation of the project’s theoretical backbone, found in the Cabinet of Curiosity.
  • Diaries, diagrams, failures, sparks — stitched together across science, speculation, and embodiment.
  • Contributions from Alchemists and Stitchers who join us along the way.
  • Agendas and rituals: the Live Lab Agenda, the Bureau Agenda, and the Observatory Agenda — mapping time and conversation across the life of the project.

This is not a clean archive. This is not a finished work.
This is imagination under the microscope.
